Programming Is Art
Poem by Tea Kawana

As if he were a painter,
he interpreted his thoughts using colour,
with his tools:
several brushes and a paint palette.
As if he played in some orchestra or band,
and the notes from the sheet music were
expressed through the very contact of his
instrument and himself;
and the translation of the incomprehensible
beautiful marks on the sheet,
to a set theme, one could envision in the mind,
was impeccably evident through play.
